About Me

Hi, I’m a passionate data analyst with a growing focus on data engineering. I believe that data has the power to create positive change, especially when it comes to tackling environmental issues — something I deeply care about. My journey so far has taken me through various exciting roles, from building data pipelines and dashboards to automating processes that make a real difference in both time and cost savings.

Currently, I’m working at WWF, where I’ve been able to streamline data quality processes and develop tools that make our work more efficient. Before that, I had the chance to work with amazing teams at EIT Climate-KIC, Amazon, and Mister Sandman, gaining hands-on experience in building ETL pipelines, improving forecasting tools, and creating insightful dashboards with tools like Python, SQL, PowerBI, and Tableau. I’m excited to continue developing my skills and contribute to projects that help solve real-world challenges.
