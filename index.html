About Me

Hi, I'm Nam.

I'm a masters student specializing in Data Science & Data Analytics.

I am a recent graduate from the Master's program Data Science and Society at Tilburg University.
In recent years, I have been increasingly concerned about environmental issues in the world. I have become very passionate about tackling these issues, not only in my free time, but also in a professional setting. My goal is to become a proficient data scientist and to use my expertise to contribute to solving some of the greatest environmental challenges.
As a data analyst at Climate-KIC, I set up a data pipeline to monitor and analyze the entrepreneurship programs offered by the organization, which aims to accelerate the transition to a zero-carbon economy. I captured and cleaned data from different sources, such as Dealroom, Salesforce, and Excel, and developed a database to store and manage the data. I also improved the reporting and deep dive processes by creating Power BI dashboards that visualized the key performance indicators and trends of the programs offered by Climate-KIC.
Prior to that, I worked as a business analyst intern at Amazon and a business intelligence and development intern at Mister Sandman GmbH, where I gained experience in building dashboards, writing SQL queries, creating ETL pipelines, and creating and improving forecasting tools with Python. I am proficient in Python, SQL, Tableau, and PowerBI, and I have a keen interest in learning new technologies and techniques.

Currently: I am working as a Data Analyst at WWF.
